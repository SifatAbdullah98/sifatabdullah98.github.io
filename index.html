Thesis Title: Advance health insurance system based on the smart contract of Etherium Blockchain.

Abstract
Every year, more healthcare insurance frauds are discovered, which is hugely worrying for society. Healthcare insurance fraud takes many forms, including fabricating data, obfuscating third-party responsibility, misrepresenting electronic invoices, etc. The present-day health insurance system needs a lot of resources and labor, severely taxing the system. Throughout this essay considering many healthcare insurance fraud schemes, we recommend a blockchain-based approach named smart contract for preventing insurance fraud. This system relies on cloud computing. And organizations like public security traffic control departments and judicial bodies. This blockchain system includes organizations like insurance companies as well. Moreover, information about medical costs, prescriptions, inspection reports, and treatment records are used in the medical process to create a blockchain for health insurance. In this thesis we propose, third-party medical process inspection, excessive medical behavior analysis and evaluation of data from medical invoices, and liability inspection services.
